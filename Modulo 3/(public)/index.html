About Us

General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an environment in which to learn to play soccer.

To be a member of NYSL you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside in Chicago northside neighborhood.

NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
